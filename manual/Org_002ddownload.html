Org-download

Org-download lets you screenshot and yank images from the web into your notes:

Figure: org-download

(use-package org-download
  :after org
  :bind
  (:map org-mode-map
        (("s-Y" . org-download-screenshot)
         ("s-y" . org-download-yank))))
